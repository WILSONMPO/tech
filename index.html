About

The Voice of
Tanzania's Tech Era

I'm Wilson Mponzi, a journalism diploma student and host of 8PM in Dar es Salaam — a live podcast dedicated to uncovering the stories shaping technology across Tanzania and East Africa.

Every night at 8PM EAT, I sit down with entrepreneurs, engineers, activists, and innovators to ask the questions that matter. My mission is simple: elevate the technology conversation in a country whose potential is far outrunning its recognition.

From fintech disruption to startup ecosystems, from policy reform to grassroots innovation — this is where those stories live.
